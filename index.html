What is the cost of goods sold calculator?
The cost of goods sold calculator is a tool that determines the cost of goods sold based on beginning purchases, inventory and ending inventory.
How to calculate cost of goods sold?
To calculate cost of goods sold you should use the following formula:
cost of goods sold = beginning inventory + inventory purchases - end inventory
Why use cost of goods sold calculator?
The cost of goods sold calculator is used in order to measure the cost of goods sold based on beginning purchases, inventory and ending inventory.
